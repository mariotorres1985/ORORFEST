What the hell is OROR?

Last year Portlanders pulled together a dozen amazing events on short notice to create a fringe-festival for XOXO. This year's your chance to get directly involved with some of the most exciting emerging projects and communities in Portland. Come to Pitchfest or follow our updates here and on twitter


Join us for a night of performances, presentations, and pitches from a kaleidoscopic cross-section of Portland’s inventive, daring underbelly. Featuring some of the favorite projects from Research Club’s 2014 year, delight all your senses with the creative output of projects founded and perfected here in Portland. And if you like what you see/taste/touch/hear, you’ll have a chance to get involved
